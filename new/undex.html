1. Storage Wars (Local vs Session vs Cookies)

LocalStorage

Persists FOREVER (until cleared).

Use for: Dark Mode, Settings

SessionStorage

Dies when TAB closes.

Use for: Form inputs, Shopping Cart

Cookies

Sent to SERVER with every request.

Use for: Auth Tokens
